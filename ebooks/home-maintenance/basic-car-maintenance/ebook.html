The Emergency Kit

Keep these in your trunk at all times:

  • Jumper Cables or Lithium Jump Starter
  • Flashlight & Spare Batteries
  • Reflective Triangle
  • Tire Pressure Gauge
  • Portable Phone Charger

How to Change a Flat Tire

  1. Loosen: Before jacking up the car, loosen the lug nuts (counter-clockwise).
  2. Jack: Position the jack under the frame and lift the car.
  3. Swap: Remove nuts, pull off the flat, and push on the spare.
  4. Tighten: Replace nuts by hand. Lower the car, then tighten fully with the wrench in a star pattern.

Jump-Starting Your Car

The Connection Order (Vital!):

  1. Red clip to Dead (+)
  2. Red clip to Live (+)
  3. Black clip to Live (-)
  4. Black clip to Unpainted Metal on dead car.
Never let the metal clips touch each other once connected to a battery!

What to Do if You Break Down

  • Get off the road as far as possible.
  • Turn on hazard lights and pop the hood.
  • If on a highway, stay inside the car with your seatbelt on.
  • Call for help from your car.
