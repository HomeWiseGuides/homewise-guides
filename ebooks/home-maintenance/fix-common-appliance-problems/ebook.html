The Golden Rules

Before you panic, check these 3 things:

  1. Plugged in? Vibrations often wiggle plugs loose.
  2. Breaker on? Check the electrical panel.
  3. Water on? Check the valves behind the unit.

Garbage Disposal Fixes

If it's humming or dead:

  • Press the Red Button: It's a circuit breaker on the bottom of the unit.
  • The Jam: Insert a hex key into the bottom center and twist to break the jam.
Potential Saving: $125 Service Call

Warm Refrigerator?

Check the coils! Dust buildup stops the fridge from cooling. Vacuum the coils on the back or bottom once a year. Also, ensure internal vents aren't blocked by food.

Laundry Room Troubleshooting

Walking Washer:

Level the feet by twisting them until the machine doesn't rock.

Damp Clothes in Dryer:

Wipe the moisture sensors (metal strips) inside the drum with rubbing alcohol to remove fabric softener residue.

The GFCI Reset

If bathroom or kitchen outlets die, look for the outlet with the "Reset" button. Press it. This fixes 90% of outlet issues without a trip to the breaker box.
