Welcome to Your Kickstart

Change doesn't happen overnight, but it can start in 21 days. This guide is designed for busy people who want to lose weight without complex diets or expensive gym memberships. We focus on small, sustainable habits that lead to big results.

The 21-Day Mindset

  • Progress over Perfection: If you slip up, don't quit. Just start again the next meal.
  • Hydration is Key: Aim for 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours to keep hunger hormones in check.

Week 1: The Clean Slate

Goal: Reduce processed sugar and increase water.

  • Drink water before every meal.
  • Swap sugary snacks for fruit.
  • Walk for 15 minutes after dinner.

Week 2: Building Momentum

Goal: Increase protein and movement.

  • Include protein in every breakfast.
  • Increase daily walk to 20 minutes.
  • Add 10m of exercise 3x this week.

Week 3: The Finish Line

Goal: Consistency and portion control.

  • Use smaller plates for main meals.
  • Walk for 25 minutes daily.
  • Exercise 4x this week.

Simple At-Home Routine

Do each for 45s, rest 15s. Repeat 2x.

1
Bodyweight Squats: Sit back, then stand.
2
Wall Push-Ups: Lean against wall, push away.
3
Marching in Place: Lift knees high.
4
Plank: Hold a straight line (knees or toes).
5
Bird-Dog: Opposite arm/leg extension on all fours.
