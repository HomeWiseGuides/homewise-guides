### Welcome to Your Kickstart
Change doesn't happen overnight, but it can start in 21 days. This guide is designed for busy people who want to lose weight without complex diets, expensive gym memberships, or endless "fluff." We focus on small, sustainable habits that lead to big results.

---

### The 21-Day Mindset
Before we dive into meals and movement, let's set the stage.
*   **Progress over Perfection:** If you slip up one day, don't quit. Just start again the next meal.
*   **Hydration is Key:** Aim for 8 glasses of water a day. Often, we mistake thirst for hunger.
*   **Sleep for Weight Loss:** Aim for 7-8 hours. Lack of sleep triggers hunger hormones.

---

### Week 1: The Clean Slate
**Goal:** Reduce processed sugar and increase water intake.

*   **Monday-Sunday:**
    *   Drink a glass of water before every meal.
    *   Swap one sugary snack for a piece of fruit.
    *   Walk for 15 minutes after dinner.

---

### Week 2: Building Momentum
**Goal:** Increase protein and movement.

*   **Monday-Sunday:**
    *   Include protein (eggs, chicken, beans, tofu) in every breakfast.
    *   Increase your daily walk to 20 minutes.
    *   Add 10 minutes of "The Simple Routine" (see below) three times this week.

---

### Week 3: The Finish Line & Beyond
**Goal:** Consistency and portion control.

*   **Monday-Sunday:**
    *   Use smaller plates for your main meals.
    *   Walk for 25 minutes daily.
    *   Do "The Simple Routine" four times this week.

---

### Simple At-Home Exercise Routine
No equipment? No problem. Do each movement for 45 seconds, rest for 15 seconds. Repeat 2 times.

1.  **Bodyweight Squats:** Sit back as if into a chair, then stand up.
2.  **Wall Push-Ups:** Lean against a wall and push away.
3.  **Marching in Place:** Lift your knees high.
4.  **Plank (on knees or toes):** Hold a straight line from head to heels.
5.  **Bird-Dog:** On hands and knees, extend opposite arm and leg.

### Quick & Healthy Meal Ideas
*   **Breakfast:** Greek yogurt with berries and a sprinkle of nuts.
*   **Lunch:** Big salad with canned tuna or chickpeas and balsamic dressing.
*   **Dinner:** Baked salmon or tofu with a side of steamed broccoli and brown rice.
*   **Snack:** Apple slices with 1 tbsp peanut butter.
