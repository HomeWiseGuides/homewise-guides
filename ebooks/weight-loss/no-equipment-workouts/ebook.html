No Gym? No Problem!

The biggest lie in the fitness industry is that you need expensive machines or heavy weights to get in shape. Your body is the only machine you'll ever need. This guide provides 50 distinct workout routines that require zero equipment.

Warm-Up Routines

1

The Classic

2 mins marching in place, 20 jumping jacks, 10 arm circles.

2

The Mobility Flow

10 cat-cow stretches, 5 bird-dogs per side, 10 deep bodyweight squats.

...and 3 more in the full guide.

Full Body Blasts

6

The 7-Minute Workout

30s each: Jumping jacks, wall sit, push-ups, crunches, step-ups, squats, tricep dips, plank, high knees, lunges, push-up rotation, side plank.

7

AMRAP 10

As Many Rounds As Possible in 10 mins: 10 squats, 10 push-ups, 10 sit-ups.

Lower Body Strength

16

Squat Variations

15 regular squats, 15 sumo squats, 15 narrow squats.

20

The Wall Sit Challenge

Hold a wall sit for as long as possible. Rest 1 min. Repeat 3 times.

Upper Body & Core

26

Push-up Progression

10 incline push-ups, 10 regular, 10 decline (feet on chair).

29

Plank Extravaganza

30s high plank, 30s forearm plank, 30s side plank (each side).

Cardio & Fat Burning

36

Burpee Ladder

10, 9, 8... down to 1 rep of burpees with 10s rest between.

38

Shadow Boxing

3 rounds of 3 minutes. Keep your feet moving and punch the air!

Flexibility & Recovery

46

Full Body Stretch

30s hold: Hamstrings, quads, chest, shoulders, neck.

50

The Deep Breath

5 mins of focused "box breathing" (4s in, 4s hold, 4s out, 4s hold).
